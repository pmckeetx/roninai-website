The "AI Shortcut" is a Technical Debt Trap

The internet is full of "AI gurus" promising you can build a SaaS in an afternoon. They are not telling you the truth.

When you use AI without a professional framework, you inevitably run into three walls:

Wall #1: The Security Gap

Your "working" app has no real authentication. Your database is exposed to the public. You do not even realize it yet, because the demo looked great. But the first time a real user signs up, you are one bad query away from a data breach.

Wall #2: The Spaghetti Code Mess

After three weeks of building, your app becomes so tangled that the AI starts breaking old features while trying to add new ones. You are afraid to touch anything. Every change is a gamble. That is not a product. That is a liability.

Wall #3: The Trust Deficit

You are afraid to put your product in front of real customers because you know, deep down, that it is held together by luck and bad logic. You cannot sell something you do not trust. And right now, you do not trust your own application.

Here is what matters: you do not need to learn how to write every line of code. You do need to learn the high-level architecture and decision-making frameworks that professional engineers use to stay out of trouble.
